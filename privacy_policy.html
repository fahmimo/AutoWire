Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Welcome to AutoWire. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our application.

1. Information We Collect

Aka Ahsan Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects information to provide better services to all our users. We collect data in the following ways:

  • Personal Information: When you sign in using Google OAuth, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect photos of vehicles, car details (make, model, year), and captions you upload to our Community Gallery.
  • Technical Data: We collect vehicle information in your virtual garage and AI diagnosis logs to improve our diagnostic algorithms.
  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication to manage user IDs and secure your data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Synchronize your garage and technical data across devices.
  • Display your profile and vehicle photos in the Community Gallery.
  • Provide personalized car diagnostic suggestions.
  • Manage your premium subscriptions and access to technical diagrams.
  • Improve the quality and accuracy of our technical database.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) provided by Google Firebase to secure your data in transit and at rest. Some data (like community posts) is shared publicly with other users of the app according to your choice to publish.

4. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can request account and data deletion through the application settings or by visiting our Account Deletion Request page.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For data storage, authentication, and hosting.
  • Google Sign-In: For secure authentication.
  • RevenueCat / Google Play Billing: For managing subscriptions.

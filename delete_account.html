Account Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your AutoWire account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Important: Account deletion is permanent. Once deleted, your garage data, service history, and community posts cannot be recovered.

How to Delete

  1. Open the AutoWire app on your device.
  2. Go to the Settings menu (cog icon) on the main dashboard.
  3. Scroll down to Data Management.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm your request.
